Sliding Puzzle

Main Menu

Objective

Solve a puzzle by rearranging tiles in correct order to make a complete image.

Rules

New Game: At the beginning of the game click "New Game" to shuffle the tiles.

Tile Movement: The game is played on a grid with square tiles. There is one empty space that allows tiles to slide into it.

Tile Sliding: Tiles can be moved horizontally or vertically into the empty space. Only one tile can occupy the empty space at a time, and tiles cannot jump over one another.

Winning: The game is considered solved or won when the tiles are in the correct order to make a complete image.

Reset and Shuffle: To reset the puzzle to its initial state click on "New Game" to shuffle the tiles to start over.

Show/ close complete puzzle: To see complete puzzle image click on "Show image", to close - click on image
